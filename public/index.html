APIMaster for Excel

An AI assistant that lives in your spreadsheet — read, edit, and explain Excel in plain language, powered by APIMaster.

Get the add-in

What it does

Understands your workbook

Reads every sheet, its structure, formulas, and named ranges, then gives a clear overview.

Edits by instruction

Write values, fill formulas down columns, reshape ranges, and clean up data — just describe it.

Formats with words

Apply number formats, styles, and conditional formatting by saying what you want.

Explains & debugs

Explain any formula in plain language and trace precedents/dependents to find errors.

Many models, one key

Choose Claude Opus, Claude Sonnet, GPT-5, DeepSeek, Kimi, MiniMax and more — switch any time.

You stay in control

Auto or Confirm mode, with automatic backups created before edits.

Getting started

  1. Install the add-in and open it from the Excel Home tab (the APIMaster button).
  2. Enter your APIMaster API key — get one at apimaster.ai.
  3. Pick a model and ask, e.g. “Explain this workbook” or “Fill B2:B100 with =A2*1.1”.

Privacy

Your API key is stored locally on your device. Spreadsheet content and prompts are sent from your browser directly to the APIMaster gateway and the model you choose. There is no separate application backend collecting your data. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
