Privacy Policy

APIMaster for Excel

Effective date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how APIMaster (“we”, “us”), the operator of apimaster.ai, handles information in connection with the APIMaster for Excel add-in (the “Add-in”). By installing or using the Add-in, you agree to this Policy.

1. What the Add-in is

The Add-in is an AI assistant that runs inside Microsoft Excel. At your direction, it reads content from your active workbook, makes changes you request, and sends your instructions and the relevant workbook content to an AI model through the APIMaster gateway to generate responses.

2. Information processed

  • Your API key. Your APIMaster API key is stored locally on your device in the browser storage used by the Excel web view (IndexedDB/local storage). It is not sent to any server we operate. It is transmitted only to the APIMaster gateway (https://apimaster.ai) as an authorization credential when you make a request.
  • Workbook content & prompts. When you send a message or use a feature, the relevant spreadsheet data and your instructions are transmitted directly from your device to the APIMaster gateway and forwarded to the AI model provider you select, solely to produce a response.
  • Local settings & history. Preferences, selected model, and conversation history are stored locally on your device to provide the experience. They remain on your device until you clear them.
  • Optional external services. If you enable optional features (e.g. web search, extensions, or MCP connections), the queries you trigger are sent to those third-party services you choose.

3. What we do not do

The Add-in is a static web application. We do not operate an application backend that receives, stores, or processes your workbook content or prompts, and we do not sell your information. AI requests go from your device to the APIMaster gateway directly.

4. Hosting and the gateway

The Add-in’s files are served by our hosting provider, which may automatically log standard technical request metadata (such as IP address, timestamp, and user-agent) to deliver and secure the site. Your AI requests are processed by the APIMaster gateway and the model provider you select, under the APIMaster terms and privacy practices published at apimaster.ai.

5. Data retention

Information stored locally stays on your device until you delete it (for example, by removing your key, clearing the Add-in’s data, or uninstalling the Add-in). Handling of transmitted requests by the gateway and model providers is governed by their respective policies.

6. Your choices

You can remove your API key, clear locally stored data, and uninstall the Add-in at any time. You control what content you send by choosing when to use the Add-in’s features.

7. Children

The Add-in is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly process their information.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this Policy: support@apimaster.ai.
